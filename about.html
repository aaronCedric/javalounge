About us

Our cozy cafe is located at the famous Bermuda Island. We started our business here because of people visiting the island. Our coffee is brewed from the finest quality of coffee beans from various place of the world. Our location is best for watching sunset as we are beside the beach. Sip your coffee on our cozy cafe and watch the sunset with your friends or family.

All of the coffees are brewed at the back of our room with the best equipments, so we can give you the best experience and a relaxing time. We blend the beans to give you a sophisticated taste, and also you can tell us to customize your blend if you prefer that.

We also produce our very own coffee beans which we blend in with other beans to maximize the tastiness. Bermuda has a humid subtropical climate and is warmed by the Gulf stream, so growing coffee beans here is perfect. The soil we use came from the volcano ashes which gives the coffee beans a distinct taste.

We roast from Monday to Sunday and open at 6 am to 12 pm.
We also take delivery orders 24/7
